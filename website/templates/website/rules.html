# Question Answer
1 What are the three main parts of an essay? Introduction, main body and conclusion
2 What should be included in the introduction? Topic, background and plan
3 How should the main body of the essay be written? Providing clear and focused sentences and using ideas from literature to support your main points.
4 What should the conclusion include? Include the most relevant and important issues concerning the topic.
5 Before starting the essay, you should make sure that? You have fully understood the question and have sufficient research for the essay
